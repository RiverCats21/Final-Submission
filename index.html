Portfolio Overview

This portfolio presents a curated collection of academic and applied work completed as part of the AI in Construction and Facilities Management course. The projects and analyses reflect a practical engagement with decision-making frameworks, risk assessment methodologies, and the emerging role of artificial intelligence in construction project delivery and facilities asset management.

Rather than treating AI as a purely theoretical concept, this portfolio emphasizes real-world use cases such as bid evaluation, risk analysis, lifecycle planning, and facilities maintenance optimization. Each project demonstrates how data-driven methodologies and structured decision-making can improve outcomes in construction and built environment management.
